## Project Assumptions

1. **Technology Stack Stability**
- WordPress version will remain stable and supported throughout the project duration
- All selected plugins will maintain compatibility with the core WordPress version
- Server resources allocated will be sufficient to handle concurrent user access and data processing

2. **Resource Availability and Expertise**
- Team members will have consistent access to development environments and necessary tools
- All team members have basic WordPress development experience
- All team members can dedicate 10-15 hours per week to the project
- Technical support from teaching staff will be available within 48 hours when requested

3. **Data Quality and Access**
- The City of Melbourne tree dataset will remain publicly accessible throughout the project
- Data format and structure will remain consistent with the current API specification
- Dataset updates will not significantly alter the core attributes being used

4. **Stakeholder Engagement and Requirements**
- Project requirements for each sprint will be finalized before sprint commencement
- Teaching staff will provide clarification on requirements within 24 hours of queries
- User acceptance criteria will remain stable within each sprint
- Feedback cycles will be completed within 3 business days of deliverable submission

5. **Timeline and Deliverables**
- Each sprint will maintain its originally planned duration of 2 weeks
- Public holidays and university breaks have been accurately accounted for in the schedule
- Integration testing periods of 3-5 days are sufficient for each major feature



## Project Constraints - Urban Tree Cover Explorer  

### 1. Time Constraints  
- **Constraint:** The MVP must be delivered within 6 weeks (3 sprints),  with approximately 100-150 total hours available for development.
- **Justification:** This timeline is fixed by the SWEN90016 course schedule, ensuring students can complete the project within the semester while balancing other academic responsibilities.

### 4. User Access Constraints  
- **Constraint:** The platform will only be accessible to the SWEN90016 teaching team and tutors.

### 5. Scope Constraints  
- **Constraint:** Future extensions (e.g., sustainability campaigns) are excluded from the MVP.

### 6. Resource Constraints  
- **Constraint:** Limited to student team capacity (no external developers or testers).  
- **Justification:** Reflects the educational nature of the project, emphasizing skill development within the team.
